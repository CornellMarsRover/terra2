Package: cmr_rovernet (rclpy)

Purpose
- Jetson-to-controller-board bridge and motor I/O handling for drives/arm telemetry and commands.

Entry-point nodes
- drivesnet_node
- armnet_node
- cmr_read_node (ccb_read)
- debug_node

Main topic IO
- drivesnet_node subscribes:
  - /drives_controller/cmd_vel
  - /drives_controller/cmd_buttons
  - /autonomy_move (legacy/alternate path)
- armnet_node subscribes:
  - /main_arm_controller/joint_trajectory
  - /mini_arm_controller/cmd_pos
  - /arm_controller/cmd_buttons
- ccb_read publishes:
  - /ccb/read
- debug subscribes:
  - /ccb/read

Launch
- rovernet.launch.py uses Fabric composition + config/*.toml.

Interaction role
- Final hardware bridge for both manual and MoveIt arm commands.
