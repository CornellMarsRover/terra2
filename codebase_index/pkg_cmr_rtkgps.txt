Package: cmr_rtkgps (rclpy)

Purpose
- RTK base/rover GNSS pipeline and logging utilities.

Entry-point nodes
- gps_basestation
- basestation_known
- gps_rover
- data_logger (gps_logger)
- displacement_logger

Key topics
- gps_rover publishes /rtk/navsatfix_data.
- basestation subscribes /rtk/rover_ready.

Networking
- Basestation and rover exchange RTCM corrections over sockets (TCP/UDP-style usage by file).

Notes
- displacement_logger currently subscribes /autonomy/pose/global/robot
  (likely mismatch with /autonomy/pose/robot/global used elsewhere).
