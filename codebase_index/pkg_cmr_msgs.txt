Package: cmr_msgs (interfaces)

Purpose
- Shared custom ROS interfaces (msg/srv/action) used across stack.

High-use messages
- AutonomyDrive
- ControllerReading
- MiniArmDegree
- MotorReadData / MotorData
- GroundPlaneStamped
- IMUSensorData

High-use services (Fabric)
- AcquireDependency
- ReleaseDependency
- NotifyDeactivate
- ActivateNode
- DeactivateNode
- RecoverFault

Interaction role
- Contracts package for both autonomy and teleop/control infrastructure.
