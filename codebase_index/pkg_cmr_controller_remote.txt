Package: cmr_controller_remote (rclpy)

Purpose
- Converts UDP controller packets into ROS topics for drives and arm control.

Node
- connect_node (cmr_controller_remote.connect)

Publishes
- /drives_controller/cmd_vel (TwistStamped)
- /drives_controller/cmd_buttons (ControllerReading)
- /arm_controller/cmd_vel (Joy)
- /arm_controller/cmd_buttons (ControllerReading)
- /mini_arm_controller/cmd_pos (MiniArmDegree)

Launch
- connect.launch.py (Fabric composition style)

Interactions
- Drives path consumed by cmr_rovernet/drivesnet.
- Arm Joy path consumed by moveit_servo JoyToServoPub.
- Arm button/mini-arm path consumed by cmr_rovernet/armnet.
